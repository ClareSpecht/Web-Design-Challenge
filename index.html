Summary: Effects of Latitude on City Weather

The goal of this project was to analyze weather patterns based on latitude. I leveraged the OpenWeatherMap to collect weather information on cities based on their latitude.

Cloudiness, humidity, temperature, and wind speed were all considered. This site takes you through visualizations of the data and explains potential trends.
